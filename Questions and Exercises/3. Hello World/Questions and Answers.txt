1. What does Babel do ?
ans: Compiles JSX into Javascript.

2. Webpack ,What does the webpack do ?
ans: A module bundler.
Solution Description
Webpack is a module bundler. The main purpose is to bundle JavaScript files for usage in a browser, yet it is also capable of transforming, bundling, or packaging just about any resource or asset. 
Further Readings: https://webpack.js.org/concepts/why-webpack/
